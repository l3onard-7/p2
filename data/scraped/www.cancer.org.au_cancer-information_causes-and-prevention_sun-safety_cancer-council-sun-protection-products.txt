Cancer Council sun protection products
Cancer Council sun protection products
Products to protect skin
Cancer Council offers a range of hats, sunglasses, sunscreen, hats and shade solutions for the whole family. The ranges not only meet Australia’s strict sun protection standards, but are fashionable and affordable.
Every purchase is meaningful and goes towards research for all cancers and support services.
Did you know that when the UV is 3 or above you need to:
Slip on clothing that offers maximum coverage.
Slop on a SFP50 or higher broad spectrum protection sunscreen.
Slap on a broad brimmed hat that covers your neck, ears and face.
Seek shade where possible.
Slide on a pair of sunglasses that meet Australian standards AS1067.
